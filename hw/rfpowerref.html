RF Power Reference

Introduction

Based on W1GHz's board, in QST April 2007 and here. I redesigned the board for SMD components and using coplanar waveguide. A comparator was also added to buffer the oscillator output.

Schematic and board were done in EAGLE. Board is currently being prototyped by OSH Park. Measurements will be done as soon as the boards are ready. Sources will be made available once I'm happy with the board, or upon request.
